Rockhounding Wiki: Geology, Rocks, and Crystals

Introduction

Welcome to the Rockhounding.org Wiki, your comprehensive guide to geology, rocks, crystals, and more. Whether you're a beginner rockhound or an experienced geologist, this resource aims to provide valuable information about the Earth's fascinating minerals and geological processes.

Geology

Geology is the study of the Earth, its structure, and the processes that have shaped it over billions of years. This section covers fundamental geological concepts, plate tectonics, rock formation, and more.

Rocks

Rocks are the building blocks of our planet. Learn about the three main types of rocks - igneous, sedimentary, and metamorphic - and how they form and transform over time.

Crystals

Crystals are solid materials whose constituents, such as atoms, molecules, or ions, are arranged in a highly ordered microscopic structure. This section explores crystal formation, structure, and properties.

Minerals

Minerals are naturally occurring inorganic solids with a definite chemical composition and crystal structure. Discover the wide variety of minerals, their properties, and how to identify them.

Gemstones

Gemstones are minerals, rocks, or organic materials that can be cut and polished for use in jewelry. Learn about popular gemstones, their properties, and where they can be found.
