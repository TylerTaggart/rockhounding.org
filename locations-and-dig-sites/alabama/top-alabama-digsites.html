Top Alabama Rockhounding Dig Sites

Alabama is home to a variety of exciting rockhounding locations, particularly for those seeking organized, paid-to-dig experiences. If you're looking to enjoy a structured environment with amenities, Alabama Gold Camp is the premier spot for rockhounding in the state. Here's everything you need to know about this fantastic dig site.

Alabama Gold Camp (Lineville, AL)

Alabama Gold Camp, located in Lineville, Alabama, is a highly popular pay-to-dig site. Here, visitors can try their hand at panning for gold, garnets, and other gems. This well-maintained camp offers tools, equipment rentals, and campsites, making it an excellent choice for families and rockhounds of all experience levels.

What You Can Find: Gold, garnets, fossils, and citrine. Visitors have a great chance to discover a variety of minerals and gemstones while panning along the riverbanks.

Whether you're a beginner or an experienced prospector, Alabama Gold Camp provides an easy entry into the world of rockhounding. They offer rental cabins, prospecting equipment, and on-site restrooms, making it a perfect destination for a full weekend adventure.

Best Time to Visit: Spring and fall are the most comfortable times to visit, as temperatures are mild and conditions are ideal for prospecting.
