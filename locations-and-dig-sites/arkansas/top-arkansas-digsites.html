Top Arkansas Rockhounding Dig Sites

Arkansas is home to some of the best rockhounding and crystal hunting locations in the United States. With its rich geological history and abundant natural resources, Arkansas offers a variety of sites where you can dig for diamonds, crystals, and other valuable minerals. Here are the top three rockhounding dig sites in Arkansas.

Wegner Crystal Mines

Wegner Crystal Mines, located in Mount Ida, Arkansas, is one of the largest crystal mines in the world. Visitors can dig for quartz crystals and experience the excitement of finding high-quality specimens in the natural environment. The mine offers tools and guidance to help both beginner and experienced rockhounds have a successful day.

Learn More

Ron Coleman Mining

Ron Coleman Mining, located in Jessieville, Arkansas, is another top destination for crystal hunters. Known for producing large quartz crystals, the mine offers public digging opportunities, where visitors can unearth stunning crystal specimens. It also features a guided tour of the mine's operations and a gift shop full of unique finds.

Learn More

Crater of Diamonds State Park

Crater of Diamonds State Park, located in Murfreesboro, Arkansas, is the only diamond-producing site in the world open to the public. Visitors can search for diamonds and other gems, such as amethyst and garnet, in the park's 37-acre plowed field. It's a unique experience where you can keep any diamonds you find!
